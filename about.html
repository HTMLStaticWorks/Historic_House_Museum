Who We Are

Preserving the Beauty of the Past

Founded in 1902, the Historic House Museum Society has worked tirelessly to protect one of the nation's most significant cultural landmarks.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in heritage preservation and immersive historical education.

Our Mission

To provide an authentic glimpse into the lives of those who shaped our regional history.

Our Values

Integrity, authenticity, and a commitment to community-wide accessibility.

A Journey Through Time

1885

The Foundation

Construction began on the main estate by the renowned architect Julian Sterling.

1920

The Golden Era

The estate hosted national leaders and became a center for cultural discourse.

1980

Public Opening

The Sterling family donated the estate to the city to be used as a public museum.
